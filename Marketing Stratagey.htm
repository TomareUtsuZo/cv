One way to organically grow Bloomer

 

I had the pleasure of talking with your Casting Manager, regarding Bloomer, and marketing. Here are my thoughts on how to organically grow Bloomer, such that its popularity and reach have an authentic value, vice a temporary faddish appeal.

 

 

Stories

People rarely remember facts or fads, past their passing out of sight. Celebrities come and go. Fads are cringed over. But stories. Stories remain with us, long after. The story of how our parents met. How our grandparents overcame great odds to give us the chance we now have. Or the story of a little girl, who just got lucky, and what she did with that luck.

We are all the main character of their own stories; and, we want others to know our story. For Bloomer to grow and build a base of passionate users, we should center our marketing efforts on the stories of our users. There are the obvious stories of our winning Bloomers. Who they were and are. Why they chose to Bloom. What has happened since. How do we connect with their stories?

We should also tell the stories of our gardeners. Those who dont and wont win the fame directly, but who go out and look for our Bloomers, and invest in their success. What was it that drew them to support that Bloomer? How did they connect? Why are our gardeners passionate about the community?

Joining a Community

We all want to belong. We want to become part of something bigger than us. When we find a community, who shares values we have, we want to know who are with. We can further increase the passion our Bloomer community feels by encouraging real space meetings. The chance to meet and share stories. To celebrate our victories, and most importantly, the sense that we are each, connected.
